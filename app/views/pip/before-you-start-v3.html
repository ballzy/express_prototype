Before you start

To qualify for Personal Independence Payment (PIP) you must have an under lying condition that affects either your mobility, physicality or mental health.

In order to qualify for PIP you must have a long-term health condition or disability that's affected you for 3 months and is expected to last at least 9 months.

  • Do you have mobility difficulties?

    Do you have difficulty moving around?

  • Do you have difficulties with simple physical tasks?
  • Do you suffer with mental health problems?
  • Has your condition affected you for 3 months and is it expected to last at least 9 months?
